## Content Writing Tips

### Projects
✅ DO:
- Lead with business impact (revenue, efficiency, users affected)
- Use specific metrics and numbers
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"
- Keep descriptions to 2-3 sentences

❌ DON'T:
- Use jargon without explanation
- Focus only on technical details
- Leave out the results/outcomes
- Write more than one paragraph

### Leadership Assessments
✅ DO:
- Explain how you apply these insights
- Connect assessments to real work examples
- Show growth and self-awareness
- Keep insights practical

❌ DON'T:
- Just list scores without context
- Include every assessment you've ever taken
- Make it sound like bragging
- Use overly complex analysis

### Publications
✅ DO:
- Include impact metrics (citations, views, downloads)
- Write clear, jargon-free abstracts
- Provide working links
- Highlight the most impressive publications

❌ DON'T:
- Copy-paste academic abstracts verbatim
- Include every blog post you've ever written
- Skip the abstract/summary
- Forget to update broken links
